GoniometerFeature

Music producers, mixing- and mastering engineers use various audio analysis and audio monitoring tools to shape and tune the sound of popular music. These include
  • Peak meter
  • RMS meter
  • Dynamic range meter
  • Gomiometer including
    • Channel correlation meter
    • Phase scope.
The GoniometerFeature extracts the goniometer data from stereo files and aggrgates its information in two values. These can be used for various music analysis and information retrieval tasks, like HipHop analysis.
